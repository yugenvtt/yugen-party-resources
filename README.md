# yugen-party (previously yugen-party-resources)

A customizable party HUD, floating resources bar, and custom pause menu for Foundry VTT.

---

### Party HUD Sidebar

A clean sidebar to track your party's health, inspiration, and active effects.

- **Layout Styles**: Choose between standard list, fan arc, or slanted persona designs.
- **Position Settings**: Move the bar to any side of the screen, or collapse it into a hover-sensitive tab.
- **Hide in Combat**: Keep your screen clean by automatically hiding the HUD during combat.

![Party Sidebar HUD Showcase](.github/assets/yugen-party-modifiers-party-ui.gif)

---

### Custom Pause Menu

Replaces the default pause screen with a stylized character sheet showcase when the game is paused.

- **Custom Stats**: GMs can choose which stats, attributes, or health pools are shown.
- **System Agnostic**: Supports different games like D&D 5e, Pathfinder 2e, and others.

![Custom Pause Menu Showcase](.github/assets/yugen-party-modifiers-party-pause-menu.gif)

---

### Party Resources Manager

A handy bar at the bottom of the screen to track shared party items like gold coins, rations, or mana.

- **Customizable Look**: Scale the size, opacity, and position of the bar, or collapse it into a single button.
- **Click Macros**: Map clicking on a resource directly to a macro or script.
- **Shared Container Link**: Link the bar to a shared loot sheet (like a party chest) to open its inventory.

![Party Resources Bar Showcase](.github/assets/yugen-party-modifiers-party-party-resources.gif)
